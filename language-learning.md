
# Language Learning: Cory's Blend

In late January 2024, I'd been sitting on an idea: maybe I should finally actually learn how to use Spanish.
Despite three years of high school Spanish that could be characterized as "an attempt to learn as little as possible", a few experiences made it clear I had still had two tenses and maybe a couple hundred words.
Thus, a new year resolution was born... except for only one problem.
Thus, a lunar new year (of the dragon) resolution was born.

I took up three pillars for my resolution:

1. become conversational in Spanish
2. as efficiently as possible while also having fun
3. without making any _monthly_ payments or watching ads
